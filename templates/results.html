Professional Recommendations
The DASS-42 is a screening tool and not a diagnostic instrument. While it provides valuable insights into your mental health status, it should not replace professional medical advice.
When to Seek Professional Help
It is recommended to consult with a mental health professional if you score in the following ranges:
  • Depression: Moderate (14-20) or higher
  • Anxiety: Moderate (10-14) or higher
  • Stress: Moderate (19-25) or higher
Even if your scores are below these ranges, if you're experiencing significant distress or impairment in daily functioning, it's advisable to seek professional support.
Future Improvements
This assessment tool is continuously evolving. Future improvements may include:
  • Integration with professional mental health services
  • Personalized recommendations based on assessment results
  • Progress tracking over time
  • Additional resources and coping strategies
  • Enhanced accuracy through machine learning models
